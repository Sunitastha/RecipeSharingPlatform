Here are a few delicious chicken curry recipes:

Chicken Curry with Spices and Aromatics

This flavorful chicken curry is made with a blend of spices like coriander, cumin, turmeric, fennel, cinnamon, pepper, mustard, cloves and cayenne. Fresh onion, garlic and ginger add depth of flavor. The chicken is simmered in a tomato-based sauce with chicken broth, then finished with heavy cream and cilantro. Serve over rice or with naan bread.

Easy Indian Chicken Curry (Murgh Kari)

This easy Indian chicken curry uses bold spices like ginger, cumin, curry powder, garam masala, coriander, turmeric and cayenne to add rich flavor to the chicken. Tomato, yogurt and onion are essential ingredients. Minced garlic and ginger provide sharpness. Skinless, boneless chicken breasts are slowly simmered in the creamy tomato-based sauce. Finish with fresh herbs and lemon.

Easy 30-Minute Chicken Curry

This quick and simple chicken curry comes together in just 30 minutes. Chicken is browned then simmered in a sauce made with onion, garlic, ginger, curry powder, coriander, cumin, paprika, cinnamon, tomato puree, canned tomatoes, chicken stock and coconut milk. Baby spinach is stirred in at the end. Serve over rice, garnished with cilantro and chili flakes. The key to these delicious curries is the blend of aromatic spices, aromatics like onion, garlic and ginger, and simmering the chicken in a flavorful sauce. Adjust the spice level to your taste. Serve with rice, naan, or mashed potatoes for a comforting and satisfying meal.
